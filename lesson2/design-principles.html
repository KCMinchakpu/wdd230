gridbyexample.com

The Grid by Example website made good use of colors with higher contrast. These are going to appear heavier and closer to the viewer, giving them more sense of importance. Brighter colors are typically going to grab the viewer’s attention much more than dull, non-saturated colors. The black color behind the text is great

microsoft.com

The Microsoft website made good use of Alignment. The Alignment creates a sense of unity by providing structure and connecting elements powerful way. The webpage used both Vertical and Horizontal Alignment, and Left and Right Horizontal Alignment very well.
