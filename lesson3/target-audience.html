Target Audience

The targeted audience for the Akwanga Chamber of Commerce includes companies, business-to-business companies (B2B), television, radio, and newspaper houses, well-educated, people looking for business opportunities, security firms, people of both genders (men and women ages 35-49 who earn more than 100,000 Naira annually.), technology stakeholders and decision-makers at companies, Business Travel Company, Facility Security Services, Athletic Shoe shops, Organic Protein Bars, Credit Union Mortgage Products, Banks, Business Travel Company, Government agencies, and so on.
