Purpose

The purpose of the Abuja Municipal Chamber of Commerce is to help business owners, Network with other businesses and professionals within Abuja Municipal and the environs so that they can strengthen the business relationships they have and increase the potential for more business opportunities. Becoming a member of the Abuja Municipal Chamber of Commerce can also help our members reach new customers. Understanding the chamber of commerce's meaning and how it works for local businesses can help you get more out of it.
